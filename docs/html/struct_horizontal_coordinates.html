◆ altitude

double HorizontalCoordinates::altitude

The altitude angle in radians.

The altitude angle is the angle between the object and the horizon, that is, 0 is the horizon, PI/2 is the zenith, and -PI/2 is the nadir.

◆ azimuth

double HorizontalCoordinates::azimuth

The azimuth angle in radians.

The azimuth angle is the angle between the object and the north direction, measured clockwise, that is, 0 is the north, PI/2 is the east, PI is the south, and 3*PI/2 is the west.
